Creamy Lentil Soup

Description

This Creamy Lentil and Vegetable Soup is a comforting and nourishing vegan delight. This wholesome soup features a velvety base enriched with tender green lentils and an assortment of vibrant vegetables. The combination of earthy lentils, aromatic herbs, and savory spices creates a symphony of flavors that will warm your heart and tantalize your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried green lentils, rinsed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)
  • Crusty bread (optional, for serving)

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and saut√© until the onion becomes translucent and fragrant.
  2. Add the diced carrots, celery, red bell pepper, and zucchini to the pot. Cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables begin to soften.
  3. Add the rinsed lentils to the pot, along with the vegetable broth, diced tomatoes (with their juice), and tomato paste. Stir well to combine.
  4. Season the soup with ground cumin, paprika, turmeric, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Stir again to distribute the spices evenly.
  5. Bring the soup to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover the pot and simmer for about 25-30 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  6. Once the soup is cooked, taste and adjust the seasonings if needed. You can add more salt, pepper, or spices according to your preference.
  7. Serve the creamy lentil and vegetable soup hot, garnished with freshly chopped parsley. Accompany it with some crusty bread for a hearty and satisfying meal.
